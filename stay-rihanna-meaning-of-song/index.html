This song is a beautiful and emotional ballad about a complicated relationship that is hard to let go of. Let’s take a look at some of the lyrics and see what they reveal.

    Stay Rihanna: music video

    The music video of Stay is very simple but stunning, as it shows Rihanna naked in a bathtub filled with cloudy water, while Mikky Ekko sings in a separate bathroom. It captures the raw emotion of the song, as Rihanna looks into the camera with a sad and longing expression. The MV also shows some close-ups of Rihanna’s tattoos, which add to her personality and her story.

    The song starts with Rihanna singing the chorus, which sets the tone and theme of the song. She asks her lover to “stay” with her, even though she doesn’t know why she needs him so much. She admits that she’s not sure if he feels the same way, or if he’s just playing with her feelings. Acknowledging that their relationship is not healthy or stable, as they are both “broken” and “scarred”, she wonders if they are just wasting time, or if there is something more between them.

    Stay Rihanna: music producers

    Stay is a song from Rihanna’s seventh studio album, Unapologetic, which was released in 2012. It was co-written by Mikky Ekko, who also sings on the track, and Justin Parker, who also co-wrote Rihanna’s hit song Diamonds. Stay is a pop and R&B ballad that talks about the temptation and the inability to resist true love, even when it’s complex and dysfunctional.

    According to Ekko, the song is very personal and emotional for both him and Rihanna, as they reveal their vulnerability and honesty in their lyrics. He also said that working with Rihanna was the “most intense experience” of his life, as they had to finish the song quickly for her record. The song was also co-written by Elof Loelv, and produced by the same trio along with Kuk Harrell.
