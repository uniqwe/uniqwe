If you have ever tried to access a website or an online service and found it unavailable, that website may have been a victim of a denial-of-service (DoS) attack. In this article, we will discover it and how to prevent them from affecting your website or services.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What is denial-of service attack<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

A denial-of-service (DoS) attack is a malicious attempt<\/a> to disrupt the normal functioning of a computer network, server, or website by overwhelming it with a flood of traffic or requests. The goal of a DoS attack is to make the targeted system unavailable to its intended users, causing it to crash or become unresponsive.<\/p>\n\n\n
